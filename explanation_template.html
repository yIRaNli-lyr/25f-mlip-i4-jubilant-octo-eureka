EXPLANATION

Why Act Now?

Save Your Driver's License

Vision loss from diabetes is often irreversible. Once it's gone, you cannot drive, read, or live independently.

It's Treatable

Caught early, this can often be managed with medication or minor laser procedures. No major surgery is needed yet.

Silent Damage

You may see fine now, but diabetes damages eyes silently. By the time your vision gets blurry, it might be too late to fix.

The Cost of Waiting vs. Acting

Acting Now

Most Health Insurance, Medicare, and Medicaid cover diabetic eye exams.

Standard specialist copay: $20 - $50

Waiting

If ignored, advanced treatment (injections/surgery) can cost thousands of dollars out-of-pocket and require monthly hospital visits.
